Terms of Reference

Njala Environmental Technicians (NET) provides expert environmental consultancy services, specializing in risk assessment, environmental audits, and hazard prevention. Our Terms of Reference outline our commitments, roles, and scope of work when collaborating with companies to safeguard environmental health and ensure regulatory compliance.

Our primary responsibilities include:

  • Conducting thorough environmental risk assessments for potential hazards.
  • Performing Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) to identify and mitigate operational risks.
  • Collecting and analyzing atmospheric data to monitor and control environmental impact.
  • Providing clear recommendations to aid companies in adhering to environmental regulations and enhancing safety protocols.

NET is dedicated to maintaining transparency, accountability, and excellence in all our projects. Our Terms of Reference are periodically updated to align with current industry standards and best practices.
