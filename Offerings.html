Our Services

At NET, we offer comprehensive environmental services to help companies prevent hazards, improve safety, and maintain compliance. Here’s an overview of our key projects:

FMEA and Atmospheric Data Collection for NMI

For our client NMI, based in Makeni, we conducted a detailed Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) alongside atmospheric data collection. This project was essential for identifying potential hazards and assessing the environmental impact in the region. Our analysis provided actionable insights to help NMI optimize their safety protocols and adhere to environmental standards.

Inspection Visit to TRADCO

During our inspection for TRADCO, we repeated our thorough analysis of FMEA and atmospheric data collection, evaluating the company’s environmental impact. Our services enable companies like TRADCO to enhance safety measures and mitigate risks. With our insights, TRADCO can more effectively monitor environmental factors and improve their compliance efforts.

Additional Environmental Services

NET is committed to providing customized environmental solutions for various industries. Our services range from hazard prevention and risk assessment to compliance consulting and data analysis, all aimed at helping companies operate safely and sustainably.
